Sandvik Cover Letter

Thank you so much for reading through my application for the role of Digital Workplace Professional. I am currently a classroom music teacher. One of my favorite aspects of teaching is creating organised, clear, well-designed content for my classes using Google's Suite of applications. These applications are very similar to the Microsoft 365 applications, and I would be a quick and happy learner of the system. When working in a team, I often take on the role of coordinator, someone who helps to organize how to put a plan into action. I am efficient and wish to make the process towards group goals smooth. In meetings, I will often volunteer to take the minutes. I work to project clear, concise, easily-understood information.

I created this website originally for my application for /Salt, a bootcamp for professionals looking to change careers into becoming web developers. I was accepted into the bootcamp program. Ultimately, I could not accept the invitation, as the 3 months program without pay was not financially viable for my family. I have since been looking for a workplace where I can provide my digital and IT skills, my capacity to quickly learn new technology, and my appreciation of working with groups of other professionals towards a shared goal. When I read the application for this position, I decided to amend the website to provide a cover letter for my application to Sandvik. While it is not important to have html skills for this position, I hope it shows my enthusiasm and commitment to learning IT to communicate an effective message.
